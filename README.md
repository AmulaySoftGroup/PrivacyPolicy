## 1. Information We Collect

The Application does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, home addresses, or precise location information.

To maintain and improve the Application, we collect limited non-personal information related to app performance and user interactions. This information may include:

* Gameplay and feature usage statistics
* App interaction events
* Session information
* Device type and operating system version
* Crash reports and diagnostic information
* Performance and stability metrics
* Device identifiers and advertising identifiers when required for analytics and platform functionality

This information is used solely to:

* Improve the quality and usability of the Application
* Understand how users interact with the Application
* Diagnose and fix technical issues
* Monitor app performance and stability
* Enhance the overall user experience

We do not use this information to identify individual users.

## 2. Children's Privacy

The Application is intended for children and families.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Any information collected is limited to analytics, diagnostics, and operational data necessary to maintain and improve the Application.

We do not:

* Collect children's names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise locations
* Use data for behavioral advertising
* Create advertising profiles of children
* Sell or share children's data for marketing purposes

All data processing is conducted in accordance with applicable children's privacy regulations and Google Play Families Policy requirements.
